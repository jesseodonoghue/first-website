How to make a sandwich

  1. Grab one slice of bread of your choice
  2. Spread condiments on slice of bread
  3. Add desired meat
  4. Add desired cheese
  5. Add lettuce and tomato
  6. Top with one final slice of bread
  7. Cut crust off
  8. Cut into heart shape
  9. Serve with cheese puffs
